Welcome to TextLeak Plagiarism Detector!

TextLeak is a easy to use system to detect plagiarism between a piece of source documentation and a corpus, an internal corpus of documents, or plagiarism between a corpus and an external Wikipedia source. The external page will be determined through a process of topic evaluation through scanning the semantics of the documents in the corpus.
